Preparing for the Future

Hayes Valley/Tenderloin/North of Market (94102), Polk/Russian Hill (94109), and Inner Mission/Bernal Heights (94110) saw the greatest increase in dispatch calls. Medical Assistance was the greatest type of incident in these areas. Greater ambulance services in these areas would mitigate the increase of these types of calls, especially in the Tenderloin area, which is in between the other two areas.

DISPATCH TIMES

Train / Rail Incidents and Structure Fires took the longest to dispatch, which is probably due to the fleet needed to combat the issue at hand. St. Francis Wood/Miraloma/West Portal (94127), Parkside/Forest Hill (94116), and Rincon Hill in the Financial District (94105) had the longest dispatch times. This is due to high levels of traffic making it difficult for law enforcement and medical personnel from going to these areas. Traffic could be diverted from these areas by building more roads or increasing presence in these areas through police stations or ambulance docks.
